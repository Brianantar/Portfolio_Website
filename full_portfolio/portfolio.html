Self-Portrait, 2005

A self-portrait sculpted out of Stoneware clay, and then painted metallic bronze.

The Bar, 2007

I designed and constructed a fully featured wet bar out of Brazilian wood, with a complete & functional drainage system, cabinets, sliding doors, and shelves.

Deconstructed Bowl, 2005

I formed a porceline bowl, then smashed it, and put it back together in an abstract form.

Snow Pitcher, 2005

Stoneware clay pitcher. Snow White glaze over Temeku for speckled effect.

Rock Sculpture, 2006

A rock sculpture carved out of an entire block of stoneware clay and fired with iron oxide.

Vase, 2005

A basic Stoneware vase dipped into an unidentified mixture of high fire glazes. An early work on the wheel.

The Violin Lamp, 2005

An old violin converted into a lamp. The fingerboard was removed and the neck of the violin was reconstructed to channel the wire.

A Slice of Earth, 2006

A meticulous sculpture out of Terracota clay and painted glazes depicting Heaven, Earth, & the Underworld. Heaven rains down into the Underworld, connecting both realms.

The "Rolls-Royce" Couch, 2007

A Rolls Royce themed couch with two built-in lamps, custom-made folding tray tables with authentic restored hinges from a 1920's era Jaguar, the Spirit of Ecstasy, and of course, the Rolls-Royce logo. The couch was painted with latex white paint, which gives the couch a leatherette feel (and it doesn't peel or flake).

Snow-Capped Mountain Dish, 2006

A Stoneware clay thrown dish, intentionally decapitated and manipulated to have a thicker, stronger presence. Snow White glaze drizzled over a Temeku base.

The first in a series. An abstract stoneware bowl intentionally manipulated, dipped in a copper Blood Red & Woo Blue glazes, and fired at over 2000Â° F.
